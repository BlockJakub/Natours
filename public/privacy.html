Your Data, Your Trust

How We Protect Your Data

At Natours, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

  • We only collect data necessary for booking and communication.
  • Your data is stored securely and never sold to third parties.
  • We use industry-standard encryption and security practices.
  • You can request, update, or delete your data at any time.
  • Cookies are used only to improve your experience on our site.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. If you have any questions or requests regarding your privacy, please contact us at privacy@natours.com.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please reach out. We are here to help and ensure your data is safe with us.
