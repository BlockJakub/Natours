Why Natours?

At Natours you don't just go to work. You are part of a mission — helping people experience the beauty of the world in a way that changes their perspective. We work in a dynamic, open environment where every voice counts.

Open positions

Adventure Tour Guide

We are looking for experienced guides for trekking and expedition tours worldwide. Requirements: min. 3 years of experience, first aid certification, and fluent English.

Travel Consultant

Help clients find the tour of their dreams. Ideal for communicative personalities with a passion for travel and knowledge of at least two world destinations.

Marketing Specialist

Create content that inspires. We are looking for a creative marketer with experience in social media and content marketing in the travel industry.

Customer Support

Be the first point of contact for our clients. We need an empathetic person with excellent communication skills and fluency in English or German.
